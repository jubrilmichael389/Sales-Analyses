Project Overview: 
This project analyzes a soda sales dataset to uncover patterns in revenue, customer demand, and product performance. The goal is to provide data-driven insights that can help improve sales strategy and inventory planning.

Objectives: 
Identify top-selling soda brands and products
Analyze revenue trends over time
Compare sales performance across regions
Understand customer purchasing behavior
Highlight underperforming products

Dataset: 
Source: Sample / practice dataset
Description: Transaction-level soda sales data including date, product name, brand, quantity sold, price, region, and revenue.
Records: Multiple rows across different time periods

Analysis Process: 
Inspected the dataset for missing values and duplicates.
Cleaned and standardized fields such as dates, prices, and product names using SQL.
Created calculated columns and measures for total revenue and quantity sold.
Aggregated data by product, brand, and region.
Built interactive dashboards in Power BI with filters and KPIs.

Key Insights: 
A small number of soda products contributed a large portion of total revenue.
Certain regions consistently outperformed others in sales volume.
Seasonal patterns were observed, with higher sales during specific months.
Some products had low sales and may require promotional strategies.

Recommendations: 
Increase inventory and marketing for high-performing products.
Introduce promotions for slow-moving sodas.
Focus expansion efforts in high-revenue regions.
Use seasonal trends to plan production and supply.
